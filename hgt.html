Har Ghar Tiranga

Home > Har Ghar Tiranga

A campaign called "Har Ghar Tiranga" is being run by Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav to get people to bring the Tiranga home and hoist it to commemorate India's 75th anniversary of independence. We've always had a more official, institutionalised relationship with the flag than a personal one. Bringing the flag home as a nation in our 75th year of independence symbolises both our dedication to establishing a better country and our act of personal connection to the Tiranga. Invoking a sense of patriotism in people's hearts and raising awareness of the Indian National Flag are the goals of the programme.

You are invited to raise the flag in your houses from August 13 to August 15 in 2022 to commemorate this historic event. Along with publishing a "Selfie with Flag" on the website, you can also "Pin a Flag" online at https://harghartiranga.com.
